OUR STORY

A family legacy built on concrete craftsmanship.

THE NYGREN
FAMILY LEGACY

NYCO Construction was founded in 1997 by the Nygren family right here in Tumwater, Washington. What started as a small concrete operation built on hard work and a commitment to quality craftsmanship has grown into one of the region's most trusted concrete contractors.

The company's name itself reflects its roots: NYCO draws directly from the Nygren family name, a constant reminder that this is a business built on family pride and personal accountability.

Today, NYCO is proudly operated by the second generation of the Nygren family, carrying forward the same values, work ethic, and dedication to quality that built the company's reputation over more than 29 years.
